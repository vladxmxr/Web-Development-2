✧ Decluttering my entire bedroom! ✧

After watching COUNTLESS episodes of Netflix original's 'Tidying
up with Marie Kondo', I HAD to make my own video on decluttering
my entire bedroom. I went from clearing out my closet and my drawers,
to ending up with 2 FULL bags of unwanted items and clothing and a
huge pile of trash I didn't even know I had in my room (ouch). I hope
my viewers enjoyed it as much as I did cleaning and editing the video.

♡ How I edit vintage instagram pics ♡

I know plenty of people are always messaging me and asking me
how I edit my instagram pictures so I chose to do a tutorial on how I
edited my pictures during the summer. In the video I discuss how I
use FaceTune's different tools, and go into every nitty gritty detail of
my FACE, and then show my preset on VSCO cam.
