Vladxmxr

My name is Vladimir Marcuta, I am 18 years old and a social media/shopping addict.

My love for social media began in 2012 through Instagram and Voto. Voto was an app used to post a poll and use up to 4 photos for everybody
to vote on (hence the name sounding like Vote-oh!) Somehow little twelve year old me would spam the app with different (and pretty pointless
polls) and make it to the featured page. every. time. Sometimes I would get a couple of hundred votes, other times 5000 other people would have
voted, boy was I a famous little Voto-ian (is that what they're called???)

Then came my shopping addiction (oh boy). With the rise of social media and Instagram bloggers/vloggers careers skyrocketing around the same
time I discovered voto, influenced me to go through different phases of clothing. Of course I peaked my modelling career when I strutted around
in my 'Thomas the Tank Engine' merch through my toddler years, but then came a more dark period in my life where all I wore was BLACK (no
I was not emo, I just so happened to wear everything black and listen to Evanescence every once in a while, please don't judge me). So those were
my toddler and younger teenage years but what do I wear now? Moving schools and where I lived at the age of 15, I ended up getting a new set
of friends who are ALL. ABOUT THE GAYS. So here came my Aliexpress phase, where all I ordered was very low quality clothing and somehow
turned it into high end fashion (don't know how, but trust me I made it work). I finally realised after a year or two that Aliexpress was NOT. WORTH.
IT (why did it take me that long? I do not know). Now I focus on more sustainable or minimalistic fashion. Only recently has my obsession for
charity shopping PEAKED and still continues to this day (I mean who doesn't want to find branded clothing at a fraction of the price???)
